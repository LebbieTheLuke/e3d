About Us

We are a 3D printing company based in Enumclaw, WA. Our mission is to provide high-quality 3D printed products to our customers.

We use the Ender 3 V3 SE, a high-end 3D printer known for its reliability and affordability. Our team is led by Luke, a passionate entrepreneur with years of experience in the 3D printing industry.
